Petitions

Leeds City Council welcomes petitions, they are an important way for you to let us know about the things that matter to you.

You can submit your petition on paper, or electronically. Before you submit a petition, please download and read the guidance on how we deal with petitions.

Please note that we no longer host new e-petitions on our website. However, you are still able to sign an e-petition which has already been submitted. On this page you can view active and completed ePetitions, and sign active ePetitions.

If you sign an ePetition on this website, you must provide us with your name, e-mail address and an address which shows that you live, work or study in this area. Your name (but no other details) will be published on the ePetition website.

If you have any questions, please contact us at petitions@leeds.gov.uk
