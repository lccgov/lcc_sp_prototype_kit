Bridgewater Place is susceptible to disruption due to high winds. During these periods, roads around Bridgewater Place may close to vehicles and pedestrians.

When diversions are put in place and it is necessary to close both the roads and footways, our staff will be on-site to assist.

Where to find information

Information will be made available through Twitter @leedsemergency.

Follow @leedsemergency

Closure maps

The following maps are available which ​show which roads and footways will be affected in the event of a closure:

  • Footway closures map(PDF, 243KB)
  • Road closures map(PDF, 832KB)

Planned work to install wind mitigation measures

Work is taking place to install wind mitigation measures on the roads around Bridgewater Place. Road closures and diversions will be needed while the work takes place.

Our interactive map displays up to date road closures and diversions around Bridgewater Place. A full screen map is available on roadworks.org.​
