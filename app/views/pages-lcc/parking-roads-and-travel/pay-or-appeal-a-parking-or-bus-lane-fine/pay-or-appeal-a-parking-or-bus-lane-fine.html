1. Pay a parking or bus lane fine

If you have received a parking fine or bus lane Penalty Charge Notice (PCN), you can pay it online.

Using our online system is the easiest way to pay, appeal and view details of your PCN.

You will need the ten digit PCN Number that appears on your Penalty Charge Notice (starting with LS), your vehicle registration number and a valid credit or debit card.

Pay a parking or bus lane fine

Discount is available if you pay promptly

Charges vary depending on the seriousness of the offence. If you pay within 14 days of receiving the ticket, the charge is reduced by half.​​​​

How much do I need to pay?

A system of differential charging is in place. This means that less serious contraventions of parking regulations will incur a lower penalty charge of £50.00. More serious contraventions will result in a higher penalty charge of £70.00.

These charges are reduced by 50% if paid within 14 days.

More information on our charging process can be found in our procedures manual.

​​​
  • Parking Services Procedures Manual(PDF, 88KB)

What happens if I don't pay?

If you haven't paid after 28 days the charge will eventually increase to a maximum of £113.

We may then take legal action against you which can involve using bailiffs to recover unpaid penalty charges, and there will be further costs to pay.​​​​​​
