    2. Appeal

    If you think you've been incorrectly given a parking or bus lane ticket, the easiest and quickest way to appeal is online.

    You will need the ten digit PCN Number that appears on your Penalty Charge Notice (starting with LS) and your vehicle registration number.

    Appeal a parking or bus lane fine

    If you have already appealed online you should have received email confirmation that we have received your appeal. We aim to respond within ten working days.

    Appeal by post

    If you are unable to appeal online, you can tell us why you shouldn't have been given a ticket by post. Quote your PCN number and include any documents that may help prove why you shouldn't have received a ticket to:

    Parking Services
    PO Box 139
    Leeds
    LS9 1AA

    Appeal discounts

    If we receive your appeal within 14 days of the ticket being issued the ticket will be frozen at the discounted amount of either £35, £25 or £30 (bus lane) until you receive a decision on your appeal.

    If unsuccessful you will be given another 14 days to pay the discounted amount from the date of the decision.

    The appeals process

    Appeals against PCNs issued by Leeds City Council are considered by the Traffic Penalty Tribunal.

    More information on how PCNs are issued in Leeds and the appeals process can be found in our Parking Services Procedures Manual.
