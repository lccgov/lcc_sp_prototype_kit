1. What you can do

Managing your council tax online is quick and easy. Register to access your account online and take advantage of the full range of services available.

You need to register to:

  • check your balance
  • see what payments have been received
  • check what your instalments are
  • choose to receive your bill by email (e-billing)

You don't need to register to:

  • pay by debit card
  • set up or change an existing direct debit
  • tell us about a change of address

If you receive housing benefits, Council Tax Support or free school meals you will need to use a different form to tell us about a change of address.
