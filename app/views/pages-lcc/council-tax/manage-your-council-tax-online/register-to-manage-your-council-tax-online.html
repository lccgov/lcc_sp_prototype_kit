    2. Register to manage your council tax online

    When you register to view your account online you can:

    • check your balance
    • see what payments have been received
    • check what your instalments are
    • choose to receive your bill by email (e-billing)

    Before you register you will need your council tax account number. Your account number can be found on your bill.

    As part of the online registration process you will request a PIN. You will need this before you can view your account online for the first time.

    Your PIN will be posted within five working days. If you need it sooner please contact us.
