    1. Report a change

    Landlords, letting agents and tenants can report changes to help us send accurate council tax bills to the right people at the right time.

    Tenants - let us know if you're moving

    If you are moving in or out of a property, let us know about a change of address.

    Landlords and letting agents - report changes in tenancy

    You will need to contact us with the following information:

    • full address of the property
    • full names of tenant/s moving in or out
    • date the tenancy starts or ends

    Please tell us if you have bought a property to rent out. We will need to know how to contact you if we need to query tenant information and where to send a bill if your rental property is empty.

    If you sell your rental property, let us know so that we can stop contacting you about that property.
