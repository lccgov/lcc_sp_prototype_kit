It is important that you tell us straight away if the name on your account needs changing.

Name change due to marriage or divorce

Provide us with your:

  • council tax reference number
  • previous and current names
  • full address

Name on your account is incorrect

Provide us with your:

  • council tax reference number
  • full name and the name as it appears on your current account
  • full address

Telephone: 0113 222 4404 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)
Email: council.tax@leeds.gov.uk

Name change by deed poll

If you have changed your name by deed poll, you will need to provide us with the following:

  • your previous and current name
  • full address
  • council tax reference number
  • copy of your deed poll certificate

You can hand these in to a One Stop Centre or post them to:

Leeds City Council
PO BOX 60
Leeds
LS2 8JR

Changing your name if you claim housing benefit or council tax support

Print a copy of the Change in circumstances form. Complete section 3, sign and hand it in at your nearest One Stop Centre or post it to us at:

Welfare and Benefits
Selectapost 15
Leeds
LS2 8BA
