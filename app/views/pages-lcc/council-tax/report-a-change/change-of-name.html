It is important that you tell us straight away if you have changed your name or it is spelt incorrectly on your bill.

  • Click to expand Housing Benefit and Council Tax Support claimants

    If you claim Housing Benefit, Council Tax Support or free school meals and you have changed your name, or the name on your account is incorrect, you must let us know by using the Change in circumstances form.

    Complete section three, sign and hand it in at your nearest One Stop Centre. You can also post it to:

    Welfare and Benefits
    Selectapost 15
    Leeds
    LS2 8BA

    • Change in circumstances form(PDF, 27MB)
  • Click to expand Name change due to marriage or divorce

    Provide us with your:

    • council tax account number - your account number can be found on your bill
    • name as it appears on your bill at the moment
    • what your name needs changing to
    • full address

    Telephone: 0113 222 4404 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)

    Email: council.tax@leeds.gov.uk

  • Click to expand Name on your bill is incorrect

    Provide us with your:

    • council tax account number - your account number can be found on your bill
    • name as it appears on your bill at the moment
    • what your name needs changing to
    • full address

    Telephone: 0113 222 4404 (Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)

    Email: council.tax@leeds.gov.uk

  • Click to expand Name change by deed poll

    If you have changed your name by deed poll you will need to provide us with the following:

    • council tax account number - your account number can be found on your bill
    • name as it appears on your bill at the moment
    • what your name needs changing to
    • copy of your deed poll certificate
    • full address

    Please do not send original documents by post.

    You can bring this to your nearest One Stop Centre, or post it to:

    Leeds City Council
    P.O. Box 60
    Leeds
    LS2 8JR
