You can pay in many ways, including Direct Debit, online, phone, post or in person.

Set up or amend a Direct Debit

If you pay by Direct Debit you can choose to pay over 10 or 12 months - you can also choose to pay on the 10th, 20th or last day of the month.

You’ll need:

  • your council tax account number (this can be found on your bill)
  • your bank account number and sort code
  • the name(s) on the bank account
Set up or amend a Direct Debit online

In person

You'll need a copy of your council tax bill. Take your bill to pay at a:

  • Post Office - pay by cash, debit card or cheque
  • Paypoint outlet – pay by cash or debit card

Post

Make cheques payable to Leeds City Council. Your council tax account number should be used as the reference number. Your account number can be found on your bill.

Write your account number on the back of the cheque and post it to:
