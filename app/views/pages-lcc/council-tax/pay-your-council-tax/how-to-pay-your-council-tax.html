You can pay in many ways, including by direct debit, online, by phone, by post, or in person.

Set up or amend a Direct Debit

If you pay by direct debit you can choose to pay over 10 or 12 months - you can also choose to pay on the 10th, 20th or last day of the month.

You’ll need:

  • your council tax account number (this can be found on your bill)
  • your bank account number and sort code
  • the name(s) on the bank account
Set up or amend a Direct Debit online

You can use this link to change your existing direct debit as well. Please fill in a new form and we will update your direct debit details.
