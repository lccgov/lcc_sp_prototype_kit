Council tax charges and bands 2017/18

Each property in Leeds is in one of these council tax bands:

Band Annual Council Tax charge
A (5/9) £826.69
A £992.03
B £1,157.37
C £1,322.71
D £1,488.05
E £1,818.72
F £2,149.40
G £2,480.07
H £2,976.09

Do you think your home is in the wrong band?

The Valuation Office Agency (VOA) is responsible for placing every domestic property in a council tax band. If you disagree with your band find out how to make an appeal.
