If you cannot pay your council tax online there are other options you can use.

Pay by standing order

To set up a standing order use your council tax account number as your payment reference. This can be found in the top right hand corner on your council tax bill. Standing orders can be set up to come out on any day of the month.

Your bank will need the following information:

Bank and branch name: National Westminster Bank, 8 Park Row, Leeds LS1 1QS
Branch address: Head Office Collection
Account number: 00000000
Sort code: 57-12-72

Pay by telephone

You can call our 24 hour automated payment line on 0113 395 7100. You can only pay by debit card and you will need your account number which can be found on your council tax bill.

If you wish to pay by credit card, or to discuss further, call our contact centre 0113 222 4404 (Mon to Fri, 9am to 5pm).

Pay by post

Please make cheques and postal orders payable to 'Leeds City Council'. These should be sent to Leeds City Council, PO Box 60, Leeds, LS2 8BB.

You must write your name, address and tenancy reference number on the reverse of the cheque or postal order.

Pay in person

Simply take the bill together with your payment to any Post Office or PayPoint. If you still have your payment card you can continue to use this. However, this will not be replaced if lost.

You can pay by cash or cheque at the Post Office. If you pay by cheque at the Post Office you must make the cheque payable to 'Post Office Ltd'. Only cash is accepted at PayPoint outlets and there may be a limit to the amount that you can pay. A receipt will only be issued on request.

If you lose your bill please call us on 0113 395 7100, we will be able to issue you with a new copy of your bill.
