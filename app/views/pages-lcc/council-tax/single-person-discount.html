If you are the only person over the age of 18 living in a property you may be eligible for a single person discount of 25%.

Apply for a single person discount

Before you apply you’ll need your council tax account number. This can be found on your bill.

Apply for a single person discount

Cancel a single person discount

If you no longer qualify for a single person discount, for example, if someone over the age of 18 moves into your property, let us know using the online form.

Before you cancel you’ll need your council tax account number. This can be found on your bill.

Cancel a single person discount

People who are exempt from council tax

Some people don’t have to pay council tax. Find out if you are eligible for a full exemption.

Second homes

You can only claim a single person discount on your main residence.

If someone moves out temporarily

If someone moves out temporarily, a discount does not always apply, even when only one person remains as we have to consider whether their main residence has changed.

For example, if two people share a property but one person moves out for three months to work abroad, the remaining person won’t usually qualify for a discount. This is because the home address is still classed as the absent person’s main residence.
