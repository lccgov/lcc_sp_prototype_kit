Set up or amend a Direct Debit

Setting up or amending a Direct Debit is quick and easy.

To set up a new Direct Debit you will need your:

  • council tax account number. This can be found in the top right hand corner of your bill
  • bank account number
  • bank sort code
  • name(s) on the account

To amend a Direct Debit you will need to fill in a new form.

Amending your Direct Debit overwrites your existing arrangements, usually changing the date or bank details.

Direct Debits can only be set up on 10th, 20th or last day of the month.

Setting up a direct debit for someone else?

If you want to set up a direct debit for someone else download and print a Direct Debit form (PDF, 0.5MB) and send it back to us.

Pay with a debit card

Pay each installment online using yor debit card or using our online form.

You will need your council tax account number. This can be found on the top right of your council tax bill.

Please note that we do not accept credit card payments online.

Make an internet banking payment or set up a standing order

Use your own internet banking service to set up a payment for the due date.

To set up a standing order use your council tax account number as your payment reference. This can be found in the top right hand corner on your council tax bill. Standing orders can be set up to come out on any day of the month.
