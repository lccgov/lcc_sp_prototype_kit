How to view your account details online and get your bills by email (e-billing)

You will need to register to:

  • see your balance
  • see what payments have been received
  • see what your instalments are
  • set up e-billing

Before your register you will need your council tax account number – this can be found on your bill.

Once registered you will need to request a PIN number which will allow you to view your account online. Your PIN will be posted to you within 5 working days.

If you need your PIN sooner, phone us:

0113 222 4404
(Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm)

Register to view your account

What else can I do online?

You don’t need to register for an account to complete these tasks

  • Set up or change an existing direct debit
  • Tell us about a change of address

If you receive housing benefits, Council Tax Support or free school meals you will need to use a different form to tell us about a change of address
