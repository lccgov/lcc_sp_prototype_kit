If you are a full-time student you might be able to get a discount or exemption.

What is a full-time student?

Your course must be one of the following:

  • a course which lasts at least one academic or calendar year and which requires you to undertake an average of at least 21 hours per week of study, tuition or work experience, for at least 24 weeks in that calendar year
  • a course which is not higher education, lasts for more than 3 calendar months, requires you to undertake more than 12 hours of study per week and you are under the age of 20

We won’t be able to award a discount if you live in a household with two or more non-students or part-time students.

Student nurses are not exempt from council tax but may be eligible for a student nurse discount.

How to apply

Use the online form if you study at any of the following:

  • University of Leeds
  • Leeds Beckett University (formerly Leeds Metropolitan)
  • Leeds Trinity
  • Leeds College of Music
  • Leeds College of Art
  • Northern School of Contemporary Dance
If you study as a full-time student elsewhere

Ask your admissions/student services department for a Council Tax Student Certificate.

You can hand this in to a One Stop Centre or post it to:

Leeds City Council
PO Box 60
Leeds
LS2 8JR

Before you start

You will need the:

  • address and postcode of the property
  • names of all adults over 18 living at the property
  • student number for each full-time student

The form will allow you to add the details of all of the students in the property so only one application is required.

Apply for council tax student discount

Proof of exemption

If you qualify for an exemption we will send you an exemption notice. Please keep this safe in case you’re asked to prove that you are, or have been, exempt from council tax.

Non-British spouse or dependant of a full-time student

If you have a non-British spouse or dependant living with you who is not a student, your student discount/exemption may still apply.

They must have a visa which prevents them from taking paid employment or claiming benefits.

You will need to let us know by downloading, completing and returning the claim form to:

Leeds City Council
PO Box 60
Leeds
LS2 8JR

  • Non-British spouse of student claim form(PDF, 27KB)

Tell us about a change

Let us know if:

  • you drop out of your course
  • someone else moves in who isn’t a full-time student
