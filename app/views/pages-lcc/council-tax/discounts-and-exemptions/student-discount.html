Apply for a student exemption

You don’t have to pay council tax if you're a full-time student.

What is a full-time student?

You must be a full time student enrolled with a recognised educational establishment and your course must be one of the following:

  • a course which lasts at least one academic or calendar year and which requires you to undertake an average of at least 21 hours per week of study, tuition or work experience, for at least 24 weeks in that calendar year
    • OR
  • a course which is not higher education, which lasts for more than 3 calendar months and requires you to undertake more than 12 hours of study. (Please note: you must be under 20 if on this type of course)

If you live in a student hall provided by your university or college then you don't need to apply.

How to apply

Use the online form if you study at any of the following:

  • University of Leeds
  • Leeds Beckett University (Formerly Leeds Metropolitan)
  • Leeds Trinity
  • Leeds College of Music
  • Leeds College of Art
  • Northern School of Contemporary Dance

Before you start you'll need the:

  • address and postcode of the property
  • names of all adults over 18 living at the property
  • student number or certificate for each student
Apply for council tax student discount

If you study as a full-time student elsewhere in Leeds

Ask your admissions/student services department for a Council Tax Student Certificate.

You can hand this in to a One Stop Centre or post it to:

Leeds City Council,
PO Box 60,
Leeds
LS2 8JR

Proof of exemption

Once we’ve processed your application we will send you an exemption certificate. It is important you keep this safe in case you’re asked to prove that you are or have been exempt from paying council tax.
