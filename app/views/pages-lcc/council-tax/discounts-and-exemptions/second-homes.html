Find out whether you can claim a council tax discount if you own or spend time in a second home.

If you own or spend time at more than one property, a discount can usually only be applied to the property that we consider to be your main residence.

Examples of the things we take into account are:

  • whether you physically reside at each address
  • the reasons why you have two addresses
  • where your family lives, if you have one
  • where you keep most of your belongings
  • whether you intend to return to one address eventually

Job-related second homes

If you are living in a second home because of your job and certain conditions are met, we may be able to award a 50% discount. Please contact us to discuss your situation.

Empty second homes

No discount applies to empty properties unless they are unoccupied and unfurnished for 7 days or less (different rules apply for properties with annexes.).

If this applies, let us know.

Register for or cancel council tax to your second home

If you have bought or sold a property that is not your main residence, let us know.
