      2. Apply

      Find out how to apply for Council Tax Support or Second Adult Rebate.

      If you are eligible to claim Council Tax Support or Second Adult Rebate, you can apply at a One Stop Centre.

      Staff will provide you with a claim form and can help you with the evidence needed to support your application.

      Complete the relevant sections of the claim form and return it to a One Stop Centre with your evidence.

      You can print off the Benefits claim form, complete and return it to a One Stop Centre or by post to:

      Leeds City Council
      P.O. Box 60
      Leeds
      LS2 8JR

      • Benefits claim form(PDF, 2.7MB)

      The New claim information booklet will help you complete the form. It will also explain what evidence you need to provide to support your application.
