      3. Report a change

      Find out how to let us know about any changes that may affect your Council Tax Support.

      You must tell us about any changes within one month.

      Changes you need to tell us about

      • wages of anyone living in the property go up or down
      • anyone moves into or out of your home
      • changes to other benefits for anyone living in the property
      • change of address
      • change of name

      This is not a full list. Let us know about any changes that you think might affect your household income.
