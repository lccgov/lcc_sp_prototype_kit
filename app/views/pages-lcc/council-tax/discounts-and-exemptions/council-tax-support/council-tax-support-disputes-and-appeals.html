      4. Disputes and appeals

      How to appeal a decision we have made about your Council Tax Support claim.

      If you disagree with a decision we have made you must write to us within one month of the decision. You will need to explain the reasons why and provide any documents to support this.

      This time limit may be extended in special circumstances, but there is a risk of your appeal not being accepted if you leave it too late.
