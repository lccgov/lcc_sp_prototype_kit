Location

Access

The nearest bus stop is twenty metres away and access to the pbrary is down a slight slope before the surface levels.

The entrance has two sets of double doors; inner and outer. The inner doors are automatic and staff can provide assistance.

Internally, the library is on one level and has no inaccessible areas. There is one accessible toilet. Assistive technology is available at this site.

There are low level areas and an Induction loop is installed on the counter.

Please contact the Library if you need more information.

Renewing libray books

You can renew a library book online

Or you can call the 24/7 automated telephone renewals line on 0845 1207271

Please have your library card or the barcode number of each item to hand.
